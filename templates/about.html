About Us

V Network was established in 2001 as North Karnataka’s first news, documentary, and ad film-making company. The motto of V Network has always been to bring hidden urban talents to light and to provide coverage of ongoing events while exploring the natural wonders of the region. V Network gained popularity during the era of cable tv, tape cassettes and CDs, and has continued to thrive in the digital media age. It has produced numerous informative stories across North Karnataka.Here's a glimpse into our journey and milestones captured in photos.
