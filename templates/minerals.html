Our Minerals Collection

Iron Ore

Essential for steel manufacturing. Grades include Hematite and Magnetite.

Bauxite

A key raw material in aluminum production. Available in metallurgical (45%-60% Al2O3) and refractory grades.

Limestone

Used in construction, cement production, and industrial applications. Available in high-calcium (CaCO3 90%-98%) and dolomitic varieties..

Quartz

Essential for glassmaking, electronics, and foundry industries. Purity levels range from 95%-99.9% SiO2.

Mangnese Ore

Vital for steelmaking and chemical production. Available in grades ranging from 30%-50% Mn. Also we supply Mn02.

Copper Ore

Essential for electrical wiring, construction, and industrial machinery. Available in grades ranging from 10%-35% Cu.

Lead Ore

Used in batteries, radiation shielding, and various industrial applications. Available in grades from 40%-75% Pb..

Chrome Ore

Critical for stainless steel production and battery manufacturing. Available in grades ranging from 1%-3% Ni..
